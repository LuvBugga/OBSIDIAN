The internet is just a large numbers of routers, switches, firewalls, and what not connected together to form a Network. 

Routers help connect networks together. 
In a simple way, the Internet is just a connection of different networks (which is done by switches and routers).
Firewalls protect your network from hackers. The firewall blocks unnecessary data (which is generally the malicious stuff like virus and stuff), and only allows the required data to pass through. 
WAP (Wireless Access Point) broadcasts the data in the form of airwaves.
A PING message is used to check if the other servers or computers are up and can be reached.
An IP address is just like a real life address where your friend can send you a message. It is a unique address (meaning no one can have the same IP address). Layer 3 address is known as the IP address. 

# Switch
- Switches connect a number of computers (it can also be printers, etc), together, forming a network.
- They are generally connected through Ethernet Cables (but we can also use Fiber Optics aswell). Like every other wire (except fiber optic) ethernet cables also use electrical signals to transmit data.
- These come in different sizes like 8 ports or 48 ports version (the  more the ports the more devices you can connect together).
## Hub
- Before switches there were hubs.
- Hubs are called dumb devices.
- They are built just like switches, and we can connect them through ethernet cables aswell.
